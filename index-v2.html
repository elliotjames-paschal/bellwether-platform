The Problem

Prediction markets are powerful.
The data layer is missing.

Markets across platforms can't be compared, most prices are trivially manipulable, and there's no standard way to identify what event a market is actually about.

01

No canonical identifiers

The same event — "Will Democrats win the 2026 CA governor's race?" — appears differently on every platform. Without a shared ID, you can't compare, aggregate, or track across sources.

02

Most prices aren't trustworthy

98.7% of political markets lack the liquidity to resist manipulation. Citing these as "what the market thinks" is misleading at best.

03

Spot prices are noisy

A market's current price is just its last trade. That can be a momentary blip, a fat finger, or a manipulation attempt. The real signal needs volume-weighting over time.

Tape API

Every market scored, matched,
and ready to use

One endpoint returns a canonical ticker, a manipulation-resistant price, and cross-platform context for any political prediction market.

◉

Manipulation Resistance Scoring

Every market includes the dollar cost to move its price by 5¢. Instantly know whether a prediction reflects deep conviction or thin liquidity.

⟷

Cross-Platform Canonical Tickers

LLM-generated ticker system matches identical events across Polymarket and Kalshi into a single canonical ID. Same event, one price.

△

Volume-Weighted Pricing

VWAP over configurable windows (1h – 24h) smooths out noise and manipulation attempts. The price that reflects sustained belief, not the last trade.

⚑

Reportability Labels

Markets flagged as Robust, Moderate, or Fragile based on liquidity depth. Know instantly which numbers are safe to publish.

↻

Real-Time & Historical

Live prices updated continuously, plus full time-series data for every matched market. Track how beliefs evolved over weeks or months.

⊞

Accuracy Tracking

Calibration metrics updated daily as events resolve. Did markets that said 80% actually happen 80% of the time? We track it.
